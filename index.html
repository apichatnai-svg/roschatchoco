Our Journey

The Story Behind
Every Bean

The journey from a cocoa tree to your chocolate takes years of quiet dedication. It is a labor of love that few truly understand — a process where patience is not a virtue but a necessity.

🌱

Planting Cocoa Trees

Every journey begins with a single seed pressed into living soil. We select the finest Theobroma cacao varieties and plant them in the dappled shade of native trees. This is where patience first becomes a way of life.

Year 0
🌳

Growing for Years

Cacao trees require 3–5 years before yielding their first fruit. During this time we tend the soil, cultivate biodiversity, and simply wait. In an age of instant gratification, growing cacao is a radical act of patience.

Years 1–4
🍈

Harvesting Pods by Hand

When pods blush golden-orange, we harvest each one by hand with a machete — carefully, so as not to damage the flower cushion that will bear fruit next season. Each pod is inspected individually. Only the perfectly ripe are chosen.

Harvest Season
🧺

Natural Fermentation

Freshly extracted beans are piled into wooden fermentation boxes and covered with banana leaves. Over 5–7 days, wild yeasts and bacteria unlock flavor precursors that no amount of processing can replicate. This step makes or breaks a chocolate.

5–7 Days
☀️

Sun Drying

Beans are spread on raised bamboo drying tables under the open sky. We rake them slowly, morning and evening, for up to two weeks. The gentle tropical sun does what no machine can — it draws moisture out while preserving delicate aromatics.

10–14 Days
🔥

Small-Batch Roasting

We roast in small batches, adjusting temperature and time by intuition as much as by instrument. The scent tells us when to stop. This is where science meets sensory craft — and where our chocolate's character is born.

Small Batch
⚙️

Grinding

Roasted nibs are stone-ground for 48–72 hours until they transform into silken chocolate liquor. We grind longer than most because we believe texture is inseparable from taste. Rushing here would betray everything that came before.

48–72 Hours
🍫

Craft Chocolate

Finally, the chocolate is tempered by hand, poured into molds, cooled slowly, and wrapped with care. Each bar carries the story of every step before it — the years of waiting, the daily labor, the love of the land. This is ROSCHAT·COCOA.
